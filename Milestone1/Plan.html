Goals

The Townsville Community Music Centre web site is intended to increase recruitment of new members and involvement of members in Music Centre activities by providing membership information on the site. Also to promote concerts to increase ticket sales and encourage performers to cooperate with concert events.

Success Evaluation

After a period of three months comparing rise in number of new members and members activities for music centre to present month records. Also comparing number of ticket sales of concerts and performers enthusiasm towards participating in concerts.

Target Audience

Diverse audience including wide range of age group having specialised taste in different genre of music form the majority of our current audience, and this is who the site is aimed at
